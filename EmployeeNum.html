EMPLOYEES & ASSIGNMENT CATEGORIES

Assignment Category Type System Description
01 Full-Time Regular Employees This type of employee typically works 32 to 40 hours and has an indefinite appointment.
02 Full-Time Temp Employees Temporary employees typically work 40 hours per week. They have an expected termination date of less than one year from their hire date.
03 Part-Time Regular Employees These employees work less than 40 hours per week, but at least 40 hours in a two-week period. They receive a prorated number of holidays per year.
04 Irregular Employees In this category, employees may work full or part-time and may work an irregular schedule. UAB Temporary Service employees are appointed as 04 Irregular employees.
12 Three Twelve-hour-shift Employees (3/12 Hour) This category is for UAB Hospital employees who are required to regularly work 3/12 hour shifts a week and have an indefinite appointment.
17 Weekend Staff Only UAB Hospital employees work 24-hours on weekends and at other requested coverage periods.
99 Requisition Employees This category is limited to payments to individuals to whom UAB expects to make only one employee payment.
06 Students These students hold positions at UAB and are usually paid by the institution, a grant, or a stipend.
07 Interns and/or Residents These are individuals usually in the medical areas, but can also be administrative residents, house staff, or other internship programs.
20 & 21 Post-doctoral Scholar Trainees These individuals receive a monthly fellowship paid from the Scholarships/Fellowships object code. They are not UAB employees. If designated as an assignment category 20, they may be designate by their departments, paid by UAB and compensated by funds from grants or other non-training sources, but will not receive additional funds from a monthly fellowship.
11 Work Study Students These students receive a percentage of their salary in work study benefits established by the U.S. Department of Education.
46 Trainees Trainees are student or other individuation appointed for a specific period of time to participate in University approved programs. There is no employer-employee relationship with UAB. The following job titles are approved for this category: Athletic Scholar; Division Occasional Award; Graduate Student Trainee, Non-UAB Student/Trainee. Trainees are not eligible for any UAB benefits or privileges of employment.
59 Affiliate Employee These are individuals that are employees of other UAB entities or companies that have a contractual relationship with UAB that require access to UAB systems to perform daily business functions. The following job titles are approved for this category: Oracle Access Only and UA System Employee.This is a non-paid assignment. Affiliate employees are not eligible for any UAB benefits or privileges of employment.
